About UKG

UKG (Ultimate Kronos Group) is a leading American multinational technology company specializing in human resource and workforce management services. Our cloud-based HCM (human capital management) solutions help organizations anticipate and adapt to their employees' needs.

With a focus on innovation, UKG provides comprehensive services including HR, payroll, time management, and culture development. Our analytics tools empower leaders to make informed decisions, while our AI-backed insights help anticipate workforce needs.
